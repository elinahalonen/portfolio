About

I'm currently completing an MSc in AI & Governance at VU Amsterdam, focusing on human-AI delegation and decision-making. Through Prismatic Strategy, I help organizations navigate complexity with clarity, combining behavioral science insights with practical strategy.

I write about AI, governance, and the challenges of thinking well in an increasingly automated world on my Substack, Artificial Thought. My current work explores how we can build better systems for human-AI collaboration while maintaining human agency and judgment.
